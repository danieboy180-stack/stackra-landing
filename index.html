The Daily Struggles of African Merchants

• Sales scattered across WhatsApp, Instagram and TikTok making it easy to lose customers.

• Deliveries become stressful: wrong addresses, late riders, repeated complaints.

• Payments are messy: screenshots everywhere, confusion between paid & unpaid orders.

• Customers ask the same questions all day: “price?”, “location?”, “delivery?”

• No clear insights no daily breakdown of revenue, growth or performance.

• Tasks, reminders and ideas get forgotten because everything is scattered in different apps.

Why Stackra?

Stackra is a modern workspace designed specifically for African merchants replacing scattered apps, screenshots, chats and manual processes with one simple, organized system.

Everything works together: sales, customers, order tracking, delivery coordination, payments, chats, tasks and insights. Less stress. Fewer mistakes. More time to grow.
